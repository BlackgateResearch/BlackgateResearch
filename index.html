Blackgate Research


Blackgate Research is software engineering house and technology incubator.
We develop web services and bespoke web sites and release our frameworks, software libraries and utilities under the GPL open-source licence.
We publish technical articles and papers both in blog form and in more formal methods (released in PDF).
We also provided a central forum and wiki for our services.
